The world has dimmed. Argo must be overthrown...

In "On With the Show", the player will direct the Magician to explore a grey, dark world, and bring forth joy and color by solving puzzles through teleportation magic.
The player will use magic to conjure items back and forth to navigate the level and solve puzzles!
The magician will transform the world as he interacts with it.
Teleportation is explored as a core element in "On With the Show".
The game emphasizes the use of teleportation to change the world around you.

"On With the Show" was developed in C# XNA.
My Role:

I was a designer on the team. I wrote the backstory and specified the setting of the game, created background art assets, and designed several skills of the Magician. I also organized a sound library of all the audio assets we plugged into the game, and playtested for/reported bugs in the game.

Controls:

Movement: WASD, Arrow Keys
Jump: Space Bar
Hat Trick: Click an item, then the Magician. The item will reappear next to the magician. Magician cannot move while using this ability.
Prestidigitation: Click on an item close to the Magician, then on a location in the level. The item will teleport to the mouse pointer. Magician cannot move while using this ability.
The Switch: Click on a nearby item and another item. They will swap places. Magician cannot move while using this ability.
Cancel Skill: After starting a magic trick/skill, right click anywhere on the stage. The Magician stops the trick and can move again.
