Stealth Elements.

The character is a vampire who can stealth. Stealthing will cost a certain amount of energy to maintain, so use it in tight spots. Enemies can't chase stealthed targets.

Realistic Lighting, Shadows.

You can't see what you aren't in range of, and they can't see you either! Similarily, if there is a torch or other light source, both you and your enemies can see further.

One shot, one kill.

You're a badass vampire, and you can kill anything with a touch of your blade. Stealth and approach from the shadows for a stealth kill, or run at them with your blade - your choice.
