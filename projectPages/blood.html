Valter's off on yet another errand.

In Blood, the player will direct Valter, the protagonist and friendly vampire, on a series of assassination missions.
Valter starts off only with a stealth ability and his sword, but will gradually pick up more and more skills along the way.
Each level has several minions, and a boss monster that will grant Valter an ability once defeated.
Proper usage of light and shadows will allow players to defeat enemies with ease.

Blood was developed in Unity3D with C#.

My Role and future development:

I'm the producer on the team. I work closely with the entire team to bring the world of Project:Blood to life. My focus for the development of this game was on having a clear communication between any developer on the team; and I encouraged having weekly jam sessions together. I kept the team well equipped and informed with frequent meetings, and partitioned work and duties between myself and other team members. We burned the midnight oil many times together, and I'm proud to be able to work with such a motivated team.

Most recently, I held a session with the team to build the lore and backstory of our game so we would have a better footing heading into the next quarter.
