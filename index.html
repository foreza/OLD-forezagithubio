About Me

Hi there! I'm Jason Chiu, and I'm a student at the University of California, Irvine. I major in Computer Game Science and am looking to minor in Business Management.
I manage and mentor individuals who wish to enter the world of game development, and learn everything I can lay my hands on, whether it be 3D animation with Iclone/3DxChange, front-end web development with HTML/CSS, or marketing and project management. I look to perfect and master everything I do.

I'm currently employed as a Campus Ambassador for Readyforce for my school, and a research assistant at the California Plug Load Research Center.
I was one of the organizers the first HackUCI; which drew over 300+ attendees as the first student-organized hackathon in Orange County, and lasted for 36 hours.

At UCI, I'm the Vice President of the Video Game Design Club, and Webmaster of the Taiwanese American Organization @ UCI.

In my spare time, I enjoy League of Legends, Hearthstone, piano improvisation, and iced coffee.
