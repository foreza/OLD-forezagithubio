LANTERN FESTIVAL AND NIGHT MARKET

Lantern Festival is an event held by TAO @ UCI every year. It's an event that is a showcase of cultural beauty and diversity. The official Lantern Festival is a festival celebrated to mark the last day of the lunar New Year celebration. Children go out at night to temples carrying paper lanterns and solve riddles. Lanterns are almost always red, symbolizing good fortune.
